### 1. Project Scope Change

- Shifted from a full store map view to a **single stitched camera overview** to simplify processing.
- Focus is now on **data processing and mining**, not real-time surveillance.
- Eliminated live processing to reduce system complexity.

---

### 2. Camera System Changes

- Still using multiple cameras.
- Cameras will be **stitched together during pre-processing** into one large video file.
- Processing will be done in **batch mode** (not live).
- The program will treat the stitched footage as **one single video source**.
- Camera angles must be **similar** for proper stitching.
- Live feed processing was removed due to:
  - High implementation complexity
  - Redundancy with existing security camera systems
  - Misalignment with product focus (data analytics vs surveillance)

---

### 3. Path & Zone Analysis

- The system will process **customer movement paths**.
- Users can define **product zones** within the store.
- Movement within zones will be tracked and analyzed.

---

### 4. Checkout Camera & Market Basket Analysis

- One dedicated camera will be placed at checkout.
- Captures customer images during purchase.
- Performs **Market Basket Analysis**.
- Collects demographic data including:
  - Age
  - Sex
  - Race
- Generates insights on:
  - Purchase behavior
  - Customer demographic trends
